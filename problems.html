What are we doing wrong?

People in recent years, do not care about their environment, as a result they creat problems on earth.

First of all, people consume a food or a drink and then they throw their trash in the ground without thinking that they will harm the environment. This trash, can be carried to the sea, with the wind, and kill the animals. For example recently the WWF team rescued a trapped dolphin, whitch was found on the shore of a sea full of plastic trash.

Also, a lot of people don't recycle their trash and this is bad for the planet, because their trash takes time to decompose. If people recycled their trash, then they could be reused.

The planet is warming faster than ever, due to the huge amount of gases that humans are releasing into the atmosphere. This is done by activities such as driving cars, burning fossil fuels and burning forests. This climate changes is causing ice caps to melt and creating new dangers such as flash floods, while reducing water supplies for hundreds of millions of people in the future.
