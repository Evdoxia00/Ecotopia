We have until 2050 to save our Earth

The environment is an important issue even when society is faced with so many problems such as economic crises, wars, social problems, etc. It matters a lot because not only is Earth our home but also it provides air, food, and other incredibly significant needs.

One of the most threaten environmental issues is that marine life has started to dying.

Marine life is facing down three huge threats: overfishing, pollution and climate change. Most of these are caused by human mismanagement. Nature is stretching to breaking point. So, if we don't stop immediately, the ocean will drastically change within our lifetime.
